Baccabiu is a cross-border craft service brand originating from Taiwan and established in Nepal. It combines the aesthetics of design with the warmth of handicrafts to create works with humanity and texture.


We collaborate with local workshops for a long time, excelling at local communication, integrating design concepts with local production resources, and practicing a collaborative model that is "scalable, co-creative, and long-term." In the artisanal production environment, which presents both challenges and opportunities, Zizhi always upholds a spirit of stability and flexibility, responding to every creative and collaborative proposal.


We believe that craftsmanship is not just about production, and collaboration is not just about delivery, but rather a patient dialogue and a collaborative creative journey.
